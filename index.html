I'm a mechanical engineer working at the intersection of heavy industry and digital innovation.

Based in Perth, Western Australia, I work in the mining sector supporting the product development, Enhancement of screen media, mills and scrubbers to improve efficiency and simultaneously developing Python and AI/ML applications on the Microsoft Power Platform to digitise wear records and enable predictive maintenance for clients including Rio Tinto and BHP.

With degrees in Mechanical Engineering, IT Networking and Project Management, and active membership in Engineers Australia (MIEAust), I bring end-to-end capability across design, compliance, data, and project delivery.
